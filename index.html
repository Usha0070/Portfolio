The Story

"
I grew up believing the best teachers don't just explain — they make you see. That belief sent me to the whiteboard and the compiler at the same time.
— Usha Khan, Basabo, Dhaka
🧪
Why Chemistry?

Chemistry was the first subject that made me feel like science was alive — not formulas to memorise, but patterns to discover. I teach it the way I wish someone had taught me: with curiosity first.

💡
Why Computer Science?

At BUET, I found a second language — one that lets you build anything you can think of. As an educator who codes, I sit at a rare intersection: I can teach technology and build with it.

🎯
What drives me

A student who finally understands oxidation states. A piece of code that compiles on the first try. Two completely different feelings — same rush. That's why I do both, every day.
